About Us

We as StarkEra Team built the Myna - Smart Krishi Network app and try to help farmers on there crop sales, weather predictions and more on Government schemes.

Motive

We as a Myna app team our mission is to provide right information to farmers regarding currently running government schemes, Commodity prices, Weather and right news which is totally based on government research for farmers.

We want to focus more on Government Schemes and whatever schemes government want to give to farmers to tell our app users which is farmers and they can get benefits from that.

We believe education and right information can make you to take right decisions so we build this platform where we give all knowledge articles by the help of are Conversation system Myna, so please remember Myna is your real hero which is actually a real friend of farmers.
